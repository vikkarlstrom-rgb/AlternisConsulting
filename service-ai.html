FAQ

Common questions

Do AI solutions need a large transformation initiative?

No. Many successful AI implementations start with a contained, measurable use case such as knowledge retrieval, internal support, or workflow augmentation.

Can AI be integrated into existing Microsoft environments?

Yes. Alternis is specifically positioned to work within Microsoft-oriented enterprise environments and integrate AI capabilities into existing platforms and security models.

How do you reduce risk in enterprise AI delivery?

By grounding responses in approved sources, establishing access control, narrowing early scope, and defining clear evaluation criteria before scaling the solution.
